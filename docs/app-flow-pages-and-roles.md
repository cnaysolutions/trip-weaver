Page Purposes (One Line Each)
1. Landing
	• Sets emotional tone and explains the value: calm, premium trip planning without booking pressure.
2. Authentication
	• Simple sign-in to save trips and return later.
	• No urgency. No gated surprise.
3. Concierge Intake
	• Conversational trip definition.
	• Captures intent, not just parameters.
4. Trip Overview
	• Big-picture snapshot of the entire journey.
	• Acts as the “concierge desk.”
5. Flights
	• Curated flight recommendations with real pricing.
	• Primary cost anchor for the trip.
6. Airport Mobility
	• Optional airport car rental.
	• Contextual, thoughtful mobility layer.
7. Hotel Discovery
	• Informational hotel options.
	• Reassuring context, not a sales surface.
8. Daily Itinerary
	• AI-orchestrated, day-by-day journey flow.
	• Time-aware and distance-aware.
9. Cost Intelligence
	• Transparent cost ledger.
	• Full control over inclusions and exclusions.
10. Trip Summary
	• Calm, complete overview.
	• Ready to share, review, or export.
11. Account
	• Manage profile and saved trips.
	• No configuration clutter.

User Roles & Access Levels
1. Traveler (Primary User)
Capabilities:
	• Create and edit trips.
	• Include or exclude any item.
	• View real-time cost changes.
	• Interact with Concierge AI.
Restrictions:
	• Cannot book directly.
	• Cannot modify provider pricing logic.

2. Guest (Read-Only)
Capabilities:
	• View shared trip summaries.
	• See itinerary and cost breakdown.
Restrictions:
	• No edits.
	• No recalculations.
	• No AI interaction.

3. Admin (Internal)
Capabilities:
	• Monitor API health.
	• Adjust “reasonable pricing” thresholds.
	• Tune AI behavior and verbosity.
	• Review anonymized usage patterns.
Restrictions:
	• No access to personal trip details unless explicitly authorized.

Primary User Journeys (≤ 3 Steps Each)
Journey 1 — Create a Calm Trip Plan
	1. Describe trip in Concierge Intake.
	2. Review curated flights and itinerary.
	3. Adjust inclusions with instant feedback.

Journey 2 — Reduce Cost Anxiety
	1. Open Cost Intelligence view.
	2. Exclude optional items with one click.
	3. See updated total immediately.

Journey 3 — Explore Without Commitment
	1. Toggle hotel or car options on.
	2. Review informational context.
	3. Toggle off without penalty.

Journey 4 — Understand the Plan
	1. Ask Concierge AI “Why this?”
	2. Read brief explanation.
	3. Continue without interruption.

Journey 5 — Share for Alignment
	1. Open Trip Summary.
	2. Generate read-only link.
	3. Share with partner or family.

Flow Principles (Non-Negotiable)
	• No dead ends.
	• No forced steps.
	• No irreversible actions.
	• Every page links back to the Trip Overview.
	• The user always knows:
		○ Where they are.
		○ What’s included.
		○ What can be changed.

Final Integrity Check
TripWeave’s app flow:
	• Mirrors how a human concierge thinks.
	• Prioritizes reassurance over efficiency.
	• Treats planning as a conversation, not a funnel.
The system never rushes.
The system never pressures.
The system always listens.
